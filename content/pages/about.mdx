## Who We Are

Gamiary is a browser-first game site focused on **free online games that launch instantly**. We collect lightweight HTML5 games across popular categories such as puzzle, action, racing, sports, strategy, and more, so players can start playing without downloads or installation.

## What We Provide

- Curated browser games with fast access.
- Category-based discovery to help players find the right game quickly.
- Mobile-friendly gameplay for short sessions on phone, tablet, or desktop.
- A simple experience centered on instant play.

## Our Goal

We want Gamiary to stay straightforward: open the site, pick a game, and play immediately. We focus on keeping discovery clear, page performance fast, and the overall experience easy to use across languages and devices.
